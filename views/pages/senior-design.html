Bird Call Display

Project Description

Objective: A non-touch sensor activated zoo display that triggers audibly heard bird calls correlated to a display of birds from the Red River Zoo and surrounding region.

The bird call display was a suggested idea from the Red River Zoo. My senior design team consisting of Tom, Andrew and myself chose to tackle this project because the idea sounded challenging and fun, we would be contributing to a local non-profit who would be gracious for our contribution, and there were no hard requirements from the client which allowed us to get creative and build the display how we wanted.

The main components of the bird call display are a durable waterproof enclosure, speaker, PIC microprocessor, audio player with microSD card, interchangeable poster, and PIR motion sensors.

The details of how we created requirements, determined the best options, and built our final product can be found in the documents below.
